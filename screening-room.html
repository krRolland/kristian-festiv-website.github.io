IVOTE
2012
Academy Award nominee Eric Roberts (The Dark Knight), Dan Lauria (The Wonder Years, Sullivan & Son) & Emmy nominee Sharon Lawrence (NYPD Blue, Desperate Housewives) star in "iVote,' the true story of a typist who exposes the sins of his own employer, all in the name of democracy. During George W. Bush's re-election campaign, Steve is faced with a dilemma when he discovers that the company he works for helped to hide that the e-voting machines were fraudulent. "iVote" reveals the personal sacrifice and courage it takes to serve the greater good.
