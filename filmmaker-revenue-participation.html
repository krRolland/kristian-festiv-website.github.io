FILMMAKER REVENUE PARTICIPATION
Platform revenue participation for filmmakers is determined as follows: A total of 5% of the platform’s quarterly gross margin revenue is paid out to all platform filmmakers with the total 5% revenue participation divided amongst all filmmakers based on the total number of times a filmmaker’s film was screened including by distributors for acquisition consideration and the general viewing audience when a film is no longer designated for acquisition consideration.  

An example of how the revenue participation payout for a filmmaker is determined can be seen below:

1. Gross Margin for the Quarter = $10,000,000
2. 5% of Gross Margin = $500,000
3. If Filmmaker A’s film has 15% of the quarterly views, Filmmaker “A” will receive a payment of $75,000 ($500,000 x 15%) for that quarter.
