ABOUT US
FESTIV – THE FESTIVAL OF FILM FESTIVALS.
Our non-stop global film festival showcases the best Independent feature films, shorts and episodic content from the brightest filmmakers and creators from around the world.   Our curated content highlights great stories across all genres and documentaries featuring multicultural awareness, the artistic, educational, and inspirational value of beautifully orchestrated performances captured on film. Throughout the year we host interactive online panel discussions featuring filmmakers, screenwriters, actors, and business leaders from around the world and each year we host an annual event where our top films are showcased on the big screen for live audiences.
FESTIV – YOUR GATEWAY TO THE BIG SCREEN.
• More than 13,000 high-quality independent films are produced each year, with little or no actual opportunity to be seen at major film festivals or earn back costs, let alone profit.
• Festiv, your source for Festival Screened Indies, where major distributors can Click, Screen, and Acquire great films, provides a full-service digital distribution network that selects and showcases thousands of talented Independent and Student Films to be screened on any device by millions for years.
